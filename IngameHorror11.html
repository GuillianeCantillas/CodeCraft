Hello! Welcome to Haunted by Programming Languages: Java Escape Challenge! This is a series of horror-themed puzzle games that revolve around certain programming languages.

For this Haunted by PL's, you are going to be using the Java Language to answer the puzzles ahead of you.

But first, let's get you geared up!

This is the text-box, this is where all the narration happens, story wise and even some 4th wall breaks.

To your left is the pixel container. That is what could be called your eyes within the story.

Below the pixel container are three buttons.

Reset - to reset the whole form.

Undo - to erase your answer.

And lastly, the Hint button which you can go ahead and explore yourself.

On top of the pixel container is your score. You may acquire different amount of points per level as the difficulty arises. So stay tuned for that.

Oh and ofcourse! The way CodeCraft's games verify your answer is by color.

Your input will change to this color if it is correct and turn to this if incorrect

Further information about the syntax presented in each puzzle will also look like this.
