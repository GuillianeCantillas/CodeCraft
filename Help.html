Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is CodeCraft?

CodeCraft is an educational platform that gamifies the learning process of programming.
Our goal is to make coding accessible and enjoyable for everyone through interactive games and tutorials.

2. What programming languages are available on CodeCraft?

We offer a range of programming languages including Python, Java, C++, and C#.
We update our library as much as we can to include new languages and other features.

3. What happens if I encounter a bug or issue on the platform?

If you encounter any technical issues, please contact our support team at techcodecraft@gmail.com.
Provide a detailed description of the issue, and we will assist you as soon as possible.

4. Is CodeCraft suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! CodeCraft is designed for learners of all levels.
Our beginner-friendly courses are tailored to introduce the fundamentals of programming in a simple and engaging way.
