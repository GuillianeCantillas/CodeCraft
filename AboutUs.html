About Us

CodeCraft began on February 7, 2024, as a simple idea that has since become a heartfelt reality. As three friends who faced similar
challenges during our early college years, the pandemic of 2021 pushed us into the isolated world of online learning, where
programming, already daunting, became an almost insurmountable challenge. From these struggles, we envisioned CodeCraft as a
way to turn adversity into something meaningful.

For our thesis, we poured our hearts into creating a gamified learning platform, designed to make programming
fun and engaging for CS and IT students at FEU Alabang.

CodeCraft is more than just a project; it's a testament to our resilience, friendship, and belief that learning should be a joyful journey.
At CodeCraft, our mission is to make learning programming fun and engaging through interactive games.

We believe that everyone can learn to code and we strive to provide the best resources and support for all our users.
Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps into coding or an experienced developer looking to enhance your skills,
we're here to empower you on your journey.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate developers committed to creating an immersive coding experience.
Our diverse backgrounds and expertise allow us to bring innovative ideas to life.
Our team's collective experience spans software development, education, and interactive design,
enabling us to ignite creativity and push the boundaries of programming education.
We're committed to fostering an inclusive and engaging learning environment,
where learning to code is both accessible and rewarding.
